WPMissionControl Plugin: Monitor, secure, and optimize your WordPress site with real-time health, integrity, and performance tracking.

== Description ==
Elevate your WordPress website's maintenance and security with the WPMissionControl Plugin. Seamlessly integrate your site with our cutting-edge monitoring system, empowering you to ensure its optimal performance and safeguarding its integrity.

With the WPMissionControl Plugin, you gain access to a suite of powerful monitoring services:

Real-time Uptime and Response Tracking: Stay informed about your site's availability and responsiveness, enabling you to address downtime promptly.
SSL Certificate Monitoring: Ensure your site's security with continuous SSL certificate oversight, keeping your visitors' data safe.
Website Health Points Analysis: Get insights into your site's health to proactively detect and resolve potential issues before they impact your visitors' experience.
Integrity Checks for Core, Themes, and Plugins: Verify the integrity of your WordPress installation by comparing file checksums, ensuring your site remains free of unauthorized modifications.
Receive alerts about any identified concerns through multiple channels. Free users receive email notifications, while premium account holders enjoy the added benefits of Slack and SMS alerts. Plus, opt for our specialized malware cleanup service to swiftly handle any security breaches.

Showcase your commitment to website excellence by displaying your site's real-time status and performance on a public Status page. Premium users can further demonstrate their dedication to security by featuring the exclusive Security Badge on their sites.

Elevate your WordPress website management to new heights with the WPMissionControl Plugin. Embrace peace of mind, transparency, and proactive maintenance in one comprehensive package.

== Installation ==
1. Install or upload [WPMissionControl](https://github.com/alexkrauchn/wpmc-plugin/archive/refs/heads/dist.zip) to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Enter your API key on the plugin's Settings page to connect your website to the system. The code can be generated in your WPMissionControl Dashboard. Refer to detailed instruction [`here`](https://wpmissioncontrol.com/knowledgebase/how-to-setup-your-wordpress-website-health-monitoring).
